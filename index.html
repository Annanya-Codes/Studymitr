Why I Built This 💡

I'm Annanya, a Class 10 student from Bathinda, Punjab. I built StudyMitr because I was tired of boring study tools that don't understand how Indian students actually learn. This app uses real AI to explain things in simple language, with examples we can relate to — chapati, cricket, monsoon. Studying should feel like asking a friend for help, not reading a textbook alone. I hope this helps you too.
